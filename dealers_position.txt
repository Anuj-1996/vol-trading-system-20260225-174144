

Dealer Positioning Engine Page

Objective
Create a page that analyzes market maker hedging pressure using option chain data and visualizes:

• Gamma Exposure (GEX)
• Vanna Exposure (VEX)
• Charm Exposure (CEX)
• Gamma flip level
• Dealer hedge flows
• Gamma walls
• Volatility suppression zones

Underlying example: NIFTY

per strike.

CHARM EXPOSURE CURVE

Plot name

Charm Exposure by Strike

= −e^(−qT) φ(d1)
((2(r−q)T − d2σ√T) / (2Tσ√T))

Charm_put

= −Charm_call

Exposure

CEX = − Charm × OI × ContractSize × S

per strike.

Plot

Strike vs CEX.

SECTION 7

DEALER HEDGE FLOW CURVE

Plot name

Dealer Hedge Flow vs Price

Goal

Estimate how much underlying dealers must trade.

Compute dealer delta exposure.

Dealer position assumption

Dealers short options.

Delta exposure

Call_DEX

= − Δ_call × Call_OI × ContractSize

Put_DEX

= − Δ_put × Put_OI × ContractSize

Total dealer delta

DEX_total

Simulate spot moves

−5% to +5%.

Recalculate delta and DEX.

Hedge flow

Flow = change in DEX_total.

Plot

X axis

Spot move

Y axis

Dealer hedge flow.

Interpretation

Positive slope

Momentum amplification.

Negative slope

Mean reversion.
