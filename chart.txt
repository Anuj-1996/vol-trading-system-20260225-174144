# Vol Trading Dashboard - Chart & UI Component Inventory

## Page 1: Market Dashboard
**Total Components: 6 Charts, 6 Metric Cards**

### Metric Cards
- **Spot Price**: Current NIFTY 50 index level.
- **ATM IV**: At-the-money implied volatility.
- **IV Rank**: Relative rank of current IV over 1 year (0-100).
- **IV Percentile**: Percentage of days in the past year with LOWER IV.
- **VRP (Vol Risk Premium)**: Spread between ATM IV and Realized Vol.
- **RV (Realized Volatility)**: Statistically calculated vol (10D, 20D, 60D).

### Charts
1. **Price & Vol History**
   - **X-axis**: Date (Time)
   - **Y-axis**: Price (₹) / Volatility (%)
   - **Detail**: Multi-trace chart showing Spot Price, RV20, and ATM IV proxy.
2. **IV Term Structure**
   - **X-axis**: Days to Expiry (DTE)
   - **Y-axis**: Implied Volatility (%)
   - **Detail**: Shows how vol changes across different maturity dates.
3. **Volatility Skew / Smile**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Implied Volatility (%)
   - **Detail**: Visualizes the "smile" or "skew" for a selected expiry.
4. **Volume & Open Interest Profile**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Contract Count (OI / Volume)
   - **Detail**: Bar chart comparing liquidity and positioning across strikes.
5. **Option Price Decomposition (Stacked Bar)**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Price (₹)
   - **Detail**: Breaks down option premium into Intrinsic, Time, and Volatility value.
6. **Greeks Rotation Graph (GRG)**
   - **X-axis**: RS-Ratio (Greek vs ATM Relative Strength)
   - **Y-axis**: RS-Momentum (Rate of change of relative strength)
   - **Detail**: Quadrant plot (Leading, Improving, Lagging, Weakening) for Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta.

## Page 3: Dealer Positioning (GEX/Vanna/Charm)
**Total Components: 10 Charts, 6 Metric Cards**

### Metric Cards
- **Regime**: Market stability state (e.g., Stabilized, Volatile).
- **Gamma Flip**: The price level where dealer gamma turns from Positive to Negative.
- **ATM IV / RV**: Current market vol vs historical realized vol.
- **Total GEX**: Aggregate Gamma Exposure (Crores).
- **Total VEX**: Aggregate Vanna Exposure (Crores).
- **Total CEX**: Aggregate Charm Exposure (Crores).

### Charts
1. **Gamma Exposure by Strike**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: GEX (₹)
   - **Detail**: Bar chart showing Put vs Call Gamma per strike.
2. **Gamma Clusters (Institutional Walls)**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Total GEX Cluster
   - **Detail**: Uses K-Means clustering to identify major support/resistance "walls".
3. **3D Gamma Surface**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Expiry
   - **Z-axis**: Gamma Exposure (GEX)
4. **Cumulative Gamma Profile**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Cumulative GEX (₹)
   - **Detail**: Shows the "Weight of Gamma" across the price ladder.
5. **Vanna Exposure by Strike**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Vanna (₹)
   - **Detail**: Sensitivity of Delta to changes in Volatility.
6. **Charm Exposure by Strike**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Charm (₹)
   - **Detail**: Sensitivity of Delta to the passage of time (Time Decay of Delta).
7. **Gamma Regime Curve (GEX vs Spot)**
   - **X-axis**: Simulated Spot Price
   - **Y-axis**: Total GEX
   - **Detail**: Identifies the Gamma Flip level and dealer hedging "cushion".
8. **Dealer Hedge Flow vs Price**
   - **X-axis**: Simulated Spot Price
   - **Y-axis**: Expected Dealer Delta Hedge
9. **Volatility Suppression Map**
   - **X-axis**: Strike Price
   - **Y-axis**: Suppression Zone
   - **Detail**: Heatmap of where dealer flows likely suppress market volatility.
10. **Gamma Density Distribution**
    - **X-axis**: Gamma Bin Centers
    - **Y-axis**: Frequency/Counts
